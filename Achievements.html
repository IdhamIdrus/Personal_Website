FGV ESSAY COMPETITION WINNER 2021

In December 2021, I was called by the FGV Organization that I got picked as a TOP 4 Winner of the Essay Competition Under Bahasa Melayu. And It's an International one!

What Is The Competition About?

The competition focuses on the essay regarding the issue of increasing climate change in Malaysia. As a writer or participants, we are required to write long essays by generating an idea suggestion, or even theory solution on how this country could potentially decrease or solve the issues. HIGHSCHOOL MAGAZINE PUBLISH

Before MCO occurred, my high school announced that they planning to find the best story to be featured in the High School Magazine. They asked any interested students to come to the office and submit their literature artwork to be checked before being confirmed to be chosen.

How I Got Picked?

I come to the office and submit my first story called SekolahKu, Burungku.

It was a story about my classmates adventure story of meeting a birds that symbolized graduation as we are near the graduation season before the fire nation attack (Pun Intended :p).

At first it got rejected several times due to so many typos, grammar error and languages issues. But after so many times I fix it,finally it got accepted! I was so happy hearing it finally got accepted not until I got a letter informing me that the contents are too long to be fit into the magazine...

I was so sad that it made my hopes downfall so hard, I was feeling sad and depressed because there was no more chance to submit the literature as it had closed the submission as the trial SPM was near... Not until the hope appeared to me once again that I get an idea how to counter it back!

I stand on my own feet by proving that I can submit my literature works even if it sounds ridiculous, How? Since we got SPM Bahasa Melayu in the Essay Section, there is always a section that people rarely take which is the "STORY" section. And that's where I prepare by grinding myself into all possibilities of stories that I could generate and write with.

It was a gambling try as the chance of people recognizing it is rare but when the trial SPM Bahasa Melayu came, I quickly checked what the essay story wanted about, and it was about "Malaysia Patriotic Theme", I took this opportunity and grind as much as possible and this is where my teachers approved that the story is indeed worthy to be featured on the magazine :D
