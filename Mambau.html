Indulge in the Sweetness of Our Ice Candies

In October 2023, we students were tasked with a group project that required us to sell an item as part of our group assignment ENT300. My group which consists of Zullfahimi, Naim, Aiman, and Me, decided to sell Ice Candies also known as Ice Cream 1Malaysia. In this business project, we decided to split where Zullfahimi and Aiman sold the Ice Candies at the campus, Naim house to house and me at the chosen shop which is the CafeRojakLipis. The flavor consists of Redbull, AsamBoi, & Blackcurrent!

On October 24, 2023, the day I sell my first ever Ice-candies, with the price of 0.50 cents each, it is safe to say the price is "Rahmah" to the community around it. At first, no one comes to try it but slowly after mouth to mouth with other communities, many people buy the Ice-Candies. At the end of the day, I got to sell over 150 Pieces of Ice Candies. This is really a good achievement for us and we are happy with the friendly community and support from the locals.
