Embark on a Catalogue Adventure!

In Semester 4, which was the same year as our final semester, we were tasked with a challenging task where we were required to do cataloging AACR2 and MARC21 based on the list of items that we needed to find, for the subject of IMD223. Together with our 4 members group which consists of Zullfahimi, Naim, Aiman, and Me, we work together to find the materials in each of our respective ways.

  • Chapter 1: Bilik Koleksi Terhad

    OOn 12 April 2023, All four of us began our adventure by visiting the Library PTAR, which is at the Limited Collection Room, where we tried to collect data on specific materials which are books like magazines and others.

  • Chapter 2: National Library of Malaysia

    On 27-29 April 2023, together with my childhood friend, Muhammad Syazwan, tagged me along on finding materials like books, cassettes, CD-ROMs, Vinyl, etc. Our first destination would be NLM, where it took us both three days as we needed to make some appointments for specific materials to get with.

  • Chapter 3: W.P Kuala Lumpur Library (DBKL)

    On 29 April 2023, again, we both went to KL Library Under DBKL as there seemed to be some missing materials that NLM couldn't provide us with, yet to no avail, this library also doesn't seem to have Vinyl and Cassette.

  • Chapter 4: Star Picker Audio Cafe

    On the same date which is April 29, we went to the Petaling JAYA Where it is rumored that this cafe had all kinds of antique items that we can progress with to collect the data and our guts were right as all the materials except the cassette were here! the service and the staff attitude are so friendly making Me want to come again someday.

    The day after that, 30 April 2023, since we missing one last material which was the cassette, I went to the nearest bundle or antique shop that I could find, and coincidentally, it had special sales where antique collections materials including CD-ROM, Cassette and Vinyl on cheap price which I ended up bought all three of it. After accomplishing this assignment, at the end of the fourth semester, I donated the materials to the PTAR library and now it is located at the PTAR Limited Collection Room! Feel free to visit it if you are a student on the Campus Rembau.
