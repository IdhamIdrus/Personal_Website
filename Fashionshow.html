Fashion Show Extravaganza

On 08 December 2023, with the collaboration of the KafeRojakLipis as our sponsors and Dr. Ida Nadia allowed me to host an event class activity at the Smart Classroom PTAR as a mark for final last semester year, called "Smart Program Class 5D" Activity. With the support of our Class Representative, Aiman Mokhtar, we host two special activities which are the Treasure Hunts & Fashion Show. Please, feel free to take a look at our memory of fashion class activities in this section. Click the Image to see the full.

The fashion show activities start on the second course after the treasure hunts activities are complete, this fashion show is simple where four groups need to pick one member to be their fashion ambassador. Some costumes and wearable items were given randomly and they were required to complete it within 1 hour in a group. Our lecturer, Dr. Ida Nadia works as an advisor and judger to evaluate and pick the best winner for the fashion show class.

Treasure Hunt Adventure

We begin with the treasure hunts as our first course activities that start around 2.00 p.m. This treasure hunts consist of three PTAR floor levels that work like escape rooms where the participants are required to solve puzzle before unlocking further to the depth of the final floor. On the final floor which is floor 1 on the PTAR, participants are required to solve the riddle that gives the password to unlock the treasure. Note that each floor will eliminate the members or cause them to wear clown noses.

At the end of the program, all classmates member including lecturers were given merchandise of 5D that included KeyChain, Calendars, and Voucher Breakfast Worth of 3 MYR by KafeRojakLipis. This program class not only unleashed the potential and creativity of the classmates but also created better bonds and teamwork between them!
